Projects

Flash Cards

Flash Cards is a full stack MERN app (MongoDB/Mongoose, Express, React, Node).


React-transition-group is used to switch between cards. Two adjacent cards must be displayed during the transition. To deal with this cards are split into even and odd components. During the transtion both an even and odd card will always be displayed.

Volcano Rush

Volcano Rush is a full stack game. It uses vanilla JavaScript, EJS templating, Node, Express, and MongoDB/Mongoose. Zombies will maintain a fair distance between themselves and stop before lava.


The player's unique ID and score are tracked in a database while they are playing.

Mountain Data

Mountain Data uses React, React-transition-group for animation, and Uber's react-vis for creating graphs. Mountain data is a graphing tool for New England Ski areas. It compares the vertical feet, price, and driving disance from Boston.
